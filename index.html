Go Green Game!!

By Muhammed Ajmal

I have created this game as an assignment for my Sustainable Engineering course. Named "Go Green," the game is designed to develop waste management knowledge in children. Through playing, children will learn to identify different types of waste and how to manage them effectively. The game's theme, "Go Green," aligns with the goal of fostering environmental awareness. By engaging with this game, children will gain valuable insights into proper waste sorting and management, helping them to adopt greener habits.Thank you!!
